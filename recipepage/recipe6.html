Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and place a wire rack on top.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together hot sauce, melted butter, white vinegar, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Add chicken wings to the bowl and toss to coat evenly with the sauce.
  4. Arrange the wings on the prepared baking sheet in a single layer, leaving space between each wing.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until wings are crispy and cooked through.
  6. Remove from oven and serve hot, optionally with celery sticks and blue cheese dressing.
