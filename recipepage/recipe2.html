Ingredients

Directions

  1. Cook the macaroni according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in flour until smooth. Gradually add milk, stirring constantly until thickened.
  3. Add shredded cheese, salt, and pepper. Stir until cheese is melted and sauce is smooth.
  4. Combine cooked macaroni with the cheese sauce. Mix well until macaroni is evenly coated.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with additional shredded cheese if desired.
