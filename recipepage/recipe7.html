Directions

  1. In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, chickpeas, spinach, avocado, shredded carrot, shredded red cabbage, red bell pepper, and cucumber.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together tahini, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  3. Pour the dressing over the quinoa mixture and toss to coat evenly.
  4. Divide the mixture into serving bowls and enjoy immediately.
