Directions

  1. In a skillet over medium heat, add the black beans, corn, ground cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until heated through and well combined.
  2. Warm the tortillas according to package instructions or preference.
  3. Fill each tortilla with a spoonful of the black bean and corn mixture.
  4. Top with sliced avocado, diced tomatoes, chopped red onion, and fresh cilantro.
  5. Squeeze a wedge of lime over each taco for added flavor.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious vegan tacos!
