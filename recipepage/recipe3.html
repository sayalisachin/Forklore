Directions

  1. In a food processor, pulse chickpeas until coarsely chopped. Transfer to a bowl.
  2. Add vegetable broth, breadcrumbs, onion, carrot, garlic, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper to the bowl. Mix until well combined.
  3. Form the mixture into patties. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Cook patties for 3-4 minutes on each side, until golden brown and heated through.
  5. Toast burger buns and assemble burgers with lettuce, tomato, onion slices, and your favorite condiments.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
