My grandpappy used to make this chili over the campfire under the Texas stars. The secret ingredient was a little squeeze of scorpion venom in the pot.-Chris

Directions

  1. Get your filthiest campfire-scorched Dutch oven over the fire the best you can situate it.
  2. Brown the bison.
  3. Add the diced onion and spices, sautee until soft.
  4. Add tomatoes and broth and bring to a simmer for 40 minutes.
  5. Add beans and cook another 30 minutes.
  6. Serve with Tabasco and a giant spoon.
